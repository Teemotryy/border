PWB

Psychologists Without Borders is an offshoot of Multi Talent Heritage & Associates, a psychology consulting enterprise which launched in 2008 both organizations have been founded by Enitan JeJelola Awojobi. PWB was borne out of a need to provide highly specialized mental well being solutions to people across ages and social classes including the working professionals, entrepreneurs, men, women, youth and children. We seek to enhance psychosocial development impacting on individual performance, productivity, work life balance and self-actualization..

PWB seek to provide both commercial and humanitarian services across governments, private institutions, families, groups and individual clientele. Our reach is the entire world hence we are unique in our range of services.
